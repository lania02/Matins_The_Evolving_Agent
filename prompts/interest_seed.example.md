## Preferred problem types
- Problems whose core reduces to a **precise, computable/measurable object** rather than a
  metaphor.
- Problems with a **falsifiable prediction reachable in weeks** and a **cheap minimal test**
  that decides "theorem or vibe" fast.
- **Contrarian framings** that invert a dominant narrative yet survive a prior-art check.
- Problems that **open into a layered framework**, not single-point fixes.

## Things to avoid
- **Metaphor-only** cross-domain mappings that import no precise structure.
- Ideas **already standard in a mature field** repackaged as new.
- Pure **prompt-engineering tricks** or tooling hacks.
- Anything needing **proprietary datasets or heavy compute** you do not have.
- **Purely descriptive empirical** studies with no formal object or falsifiable mechanism.
